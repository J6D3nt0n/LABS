```Bash
nmap -sV -sC -sS -T 5 -n -Pn -p- 172.17.0.2
```

```Bash
gobuster dir -u http://172.17.0.2/ -w /usr/share/wordlists/dirbuster/directory-list-lowercase-2.3-medium.txt -x html,php,py,sh,txt
```
